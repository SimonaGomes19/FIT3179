World Happiness Index

This visualisation is all about showcasing how happy and content people are in different countries. It looks at various factors like income, social support, life expectancy, freedom to make choices,generosity, corruption and dystopia levels to figure out the happiness levels of people in their respective country.

World Happiness map

The countries with the highest happiness score are mostly in Europe and the countries with the least happiness scores are mostly located in Africa.

Happiness score of the top 10 countries(2019-2023)

Finland has had the highest happiness index from 2019-2023. Switzerland has dropped its happiness score where as Israel has increased its happiness score.

The seven factors affecting the happiness score of the top 10 countries

Israel has the highest healthy life expectancy but is the lowest in generosity and freedom to make life choices. Even though Finland has the highest happiness score, only its social support and freedom to make life choices is the highest among the seven factors.
